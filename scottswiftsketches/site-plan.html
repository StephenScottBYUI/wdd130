Content

Home page

Welcome to Scott Swift Sketches. This is a website to highlight the various projects, designs, compositions, and ads that I have created. Please take time to enjoy my process and my creations. If you have any question please feel free to reach out via email.

Images for the Home page

Artwork

Here is the artwork I have created. Some of it was for a class and some of it was for my family or friends. Growing up I never considered that I myself would have a relationship with art. That relationship started by me needing an outlet for my emotions after the passing of my brother. I started by playing D&D, which progressed to painting minis and then drawing what my characters would look like. I have found a passion for graphic design and using my love for computers to make the artwork I that expresses what I think and feel in the moment.

Images for the Artwork page

Logo Designs

Logo design is my main interest with graphic design. I love the idea of creating the face of company. This is an area that I will keep growing in and studying. I want to find out how to create something so iconic that it will forever be related to a successful company.

Images for the Page 3

Advertisements

I think a highlight of my career would be to create a full page advertisement for a prolific brand. Something where I created the whole thing. The artwork, the typography, the layout. The trust a company would have to put in me is immense. Right now this page is filled with some artwork that could have been an advertisement. The typography poster was an assignment I had in class to create an advertisement that showcased the font I had chosen. I had so much fun playing with heirchy of the size of the fonts. Having the viewer follow the lines of a pyramid everywhere they looked. It is one of the compositions that I am most proud of.
