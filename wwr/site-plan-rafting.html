Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the Rapid Raft Express website is to provide a place where our potential customers can land on when they are searching for a rafting Company in our area. This website should provide information about our tours such as dropoff and pickup locations, safety information, tour variations, etc. The website should also provide a convient place for customers to reserve spots and pre-pay for tours.

Audience

The audience for this website is for adventure minded, outdoor types. People who don't mind a little hard work and some team work. They will typically be between the ages of 15 and 45. They will be looking for something fun to do during a camping trip or a vacation to the area. They will want a well reviewed rafting company to provide a tour with an easy to use booking system and a way to prepay online for the experience. This will most likely be accessed from a phone or laptop. They will typically land here via a search on the internet, most likely google.
